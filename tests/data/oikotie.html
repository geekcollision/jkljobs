Designer, Software Applications Testing
 Polar Electro Oy

Julkaistu 19.5.2014

We are now looking for the following professional

Designer, Software Applications Testing

Your task will mainly be focused on testing of Web and mobile software applications. The work will include test planning, test case creation, test scripting and test execution. You will work in the Verification & Validation team in our Product & Services organization.

We expect you to have:

- At least 3 years of experience in testing of Web and mobile applications
- Knowledge of Agile methods
- Solid experience in Web and mobile applications test automation and test automation tools
- Fluent written and spoken English and Finnish

The position is located at our headquarters in Kempele or in Jyväskylä, Finland.

For further information please contact Mr. Petri Honkala, tel. +358 (0)8 5202 100. We kindly ask you to send your application with salary request to anne.petajajarvi@polar.com by the 25th of May 2014.

Polar Electro Oy

Polar is one of the world's top sports training brands. Founded in Finland in 1977, we help anyone who wants to get more from their training, whether they want to run faster, cycle further, lose a few kilos, or win world titles. We have a great line-up of products, apps and services and continue to evolve to offer the best possible experience to the people, teams, schools and clubs who use our products every day, all around the world.

If you join us, you'll be part of an international team in an innovative company with a simple mission: to help people get fitter, faster.
